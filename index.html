My name is Imogen. I'm a queer transwoman and software engineer.

In a professional setting I love to think about user experience, solving real business problems and improving process.

Whether it's an API, CLI tool or a JavaScript SPA, I put effort into building something that's a joy to use. It's worth spending extra time to make something intuitive. Playfulness can be a good!

Many of the software engineers I've worked with show deep creativity and talent when given the opportunity to express themselves. I believe in teamwork and communication first and foremost but have done my best work when given the freedom to solve key business problems without a roadmap or Kanban board.

When something goes wrong I'm quick to blame process and slow to blame people. Most people want to do good work - how can we enable them? When a production outage occurs, how can we prevent that problem from resurfacing?

Outside of work I love rock climbing, organic chemistry, making music and learning languages. I play card games semi-competitively and have placed several times within the top 500 players in North America (Hearthstone, Magic: The Gathering). My favorite chess opening is the fried liver attack.
