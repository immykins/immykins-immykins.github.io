My name is Imogen McGough (im-uh-jin mig-yo). I'm a queer transfemme and software engineer.

In a professional context I'm passionate about creating great user experiences, whether I'm designing an API, a CLI tool or a React SPA. Engineers should work hard so that their users don't need to.

I enjoy solving ambiguous problems. I'm comfortable working through a Kanban board with a team but I've done my best work when given the freedom to pursue key business goals without a roadmap.

I love to teach others and enthusiastically share what excites me: a new framework, language or design pattern.

Outside of work I love rock climbing, organic chemistry, making music and learning languages. I play card games semi-competitively and have placed several times within the top 500 players in North America (Hearthstone, Magic: The Gathering). My favorite chess opening is the fried liver attack.
